ABOUT


  • Dishayan, the dramatics club is the largest of all university clubs and stands with an enrollment of 500 members and is growing every year.
  • The aim of Dishayan is to provide students a forum for expressing themselves through their talent of acting, performing, creative writing, directing, managing and giving technical support to a theatrical project. Drama is a very powerful source for personality development.
  • Dishayan has a vision of making confident people out of every single member who is connected to the club. The club activities and drills are designed to enhance communication skills as well as interactive approach.
  • It also tries to sensitize them on social issues through the selection of such plays for enactment that draw our attention to the ills that plague our society.
  • Dishayan club majorly works on socio-political issues through nukkad natak and stage plays. The mission of the club is always to deliver something that is entertaining enough for society to enjoy, informative enough for society to grasp, and responsible enough for society to be affected.

EVENTS/ACTIVITIES



  • Dishayan has held competitions of skits, street plays and dramatic endorsements along with one or two act plays in Hindi and English and organized a lecture by eminent Israeli playwright and film-screenplay writer Motti Lerner. It also emerged as the first club of the university to put up a show outside of the university when it staged a play at the Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi. The club intends to continue with its activities mentioned above.

  • The club stages a full length play and nukkad natak twice every year in the university and also perform the same in dramatics circuit of Greater Noida and Noida that includes universities and competitive events. Moreover the club also covers events like skit/mime competitions, mono-act, act on the spot etc.

  • Club activities include workshops and interactive sessions that consist of exercises to improve stage presentation, personality development and the way to express.
